Help
1.Ticket Verification: Always carry a valid ticket or a confirmed train reservation. Be prepared to show it during ticket inspection.
2.Platform Information: Check the platform number from the station’s information board or announcements. Confirm your train's departure platform before heading to it.
3.Arrive Early: Arrive at least 30-45 minutes before your train's departure time to ensure enough time for security checks and boarding.
4.Luggage Handling: Carry minimal luggage or use the designated luggage counters for checking in larger items. Keep your belongings close.
5.Security Check: All passengers must go through security checks. Ensure that your luggage complies with safety norms.
6.Keep Essential Items Accessible: Keep identification, tickets, and valuables handy for easy access and verification.
7.Respect Train Etiquette: Be mindful of the train's cleanliness and fellow passengers. Avoid blocking aisles or doorways.
8.Boarding: Board the train only once it has come to a complete stop. Ensure you're at the correct coach (confirm with your ticket).
9.Platform Discipline: Stand behind the safety line on platforms. Avoid rushing or pushing when the train arrives.
10.Station Assistance: If you need any help or directions, approach railway staff or use the information booths available.
11.Follow Local Rules: Abide by the local station rules and regulations to ensure a safe and smooth journey.
